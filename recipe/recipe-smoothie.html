LET'S MAKE A REFRESHING STRAWBERRY OATMEAL SMOTHIE! YAY!

This is a fast and filling smoothie with a deep-pink color and a rich, creamy texture.

INGREDIENTS:
  • rolled oats
  • chia seeds
  • greek yogurt
  • banana, broken to chunks
  • almond milk
  • frozen strawberries
  • vanilla extract
RECIPE
  1. Blend oats and chia seeds together in a blender to a fine consistency. Add strawberries, yogurt, banana, almond milk, and vanilla extract; blend until smooth.
